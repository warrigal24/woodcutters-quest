The Woodcutter's Quest
By Shaun McClure & Garry Francis
For Z-code interpreters

Solution by Garry Francis
----------------------------------------
This is a minimal solution. You'll need to EXAMINE lots of things for this solution to make sense. Some optional (but significant) commands are shown in parentheses. These can be omitted once you know what to do.

As the game progresses, you will need to light a fire. There are four sources of wood for the fire: the logs, the firewood, the chopped wood and the rotten timber. I've opted for the first one in the solution, as this is the first one you encounter. The commands to find the others are shown in parentheses.

You will also need to cut a rope. There are two sources of sharp implements: the axe and the broken pottery. I've opted for the first, as it's the least obscure. The commands to use the second are shown in parentheses.

Finally, you will need to do some digging. There are two digging implements: the shovel and the broken pottery. Once again, I've opted for the first, as it's the least obscure. The commands to use the second are shown in parentheses.
